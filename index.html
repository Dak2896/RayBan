INTRO

The book features more than 50 photographs that capture the spirit and influence of the most iconic worldwide eyewear brand. Ray-Ban. These rare photographs of some of most iconic musicians, artists, influencers and pioneers showcase how the brand has spontaneously been at the forefront of style and culture since its debut in 1937 and keep continuing to being top of mind of the most iconic personalities all over the world. A picture, the saying goes, speaks a thousand words.The images reveal the special connection between the brand and some key personalities that have the courage to be themselves. That courage that is not the absence of fear, but the triumph over it, that is not just physical bravery but is what pushes people to face their fears and to go beyond their limits. The protagonists of these shots are people that are being courageous, because they have the courage to take decisions or to stand up for what is right, to act in accordance with their beliefs, in spite of criticism, thus fighting against the society and its stereotypes, they go beyond their limits and are brave to express and expose themselves, their talents, their emotions
